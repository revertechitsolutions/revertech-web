About Us

Revertech IT Solutions Pvt Ltd is a budding edtech startup that provides various services such as as online and offline internships, workshops ,placement training, project support and faculty training.Apart from this, we provide digital marketing, app and web development services to people.

Mission

To prepare the students for the next competitive world by providing quality education at low cost.

Vision

To provide quality software and hardware services to the people and thereby investing in education so that it is accessible to all.
